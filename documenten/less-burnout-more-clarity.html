Recognizable challenges for HR and management

In knowledge-intensive organizations we see the same pattern recurring. This demo and accompanying materials have been developed to make exactly these problems discussable and measurable.

Burnout and absenteeism continue to rise

Despite wellness programs, long-term absenteeism remains high. The brain stays "on", even outside working hours. The demo literally shows how (un)recoverable that pattern is.

Lots of talk, little objective measurement

HR receives many conversations, surveys and opinions, but little hard neurophysiological data. With EEG coherence and brainwaves we add an objective layer.

Talent wants focus and growth, not tricks

Highly educated employees see through superficial "wellness". A demo with brainwave data and scientific backing aligns with their intelligence and pragmatism.

What do we show in the EEG demo?

In 20–30 minutes your organization gets a visual experience of stress versus deep recovery in the brain, guided by expert explanations.

Session structure

  • 1. Baseline measurement: brain activity in normal, busy work mode.
  • 2. Transition: how the brain responds as soon as the participant begins Transcendental Meditation.
  • 3. Deep rest: increase in coherence and favorable brainwaves during TM.
  • 4. Back to action: how the brain remains in an alert but calm state.
  • 5. Translation to your organization: implications for burnout, functioning and learning.
