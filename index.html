Can we improve our brain? Make it better?

A further step: who is behind this?

The team that discovered that we can indeed enlarge the capacity of our brains. Everyone's.

A group of brain performance expert-scientists have found out how. They compared 20 years of research of EEG (brain wave patterns) research on a method to easily improve a person's brain functioning. Easily yes. Without any effort. That anyone can do.

No: no medication needed, not by using drugs. Not by implants. No change of lifestyle needed: no belief or conviction needed even. One practices by sitting comfortably with the eyes closed and practice mentally, at home or at the office.

They claim to have found a very efficient, easy to practice method, to make one's brain perform much more efficient, starting to use its full potential. And then increase creativity, intelligence, understanding things quicker, absorb information faster. And: Make one more stress resilient.

Applicable to everyone, and to every organization that wants to cope with burnout, fatigue, underperformance. Or for organisations searching clever brains to employ, facing stiff competition, thus prepare for innovative, game-changing technologies.
